# User Manual

This manual provides guidance for users of the Mini-ERP system, covering all core functionalities.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the application URL
2. Enter your username and password in the login form
3. Click the "Login" button

### Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a quick overview of key metrics and recent activities. You can customize the dashboard view based on your role.

## Core Functionalities

### Product Management

#### Adding New Products

1. Navigate to the "Products" section
2. Click the "Add Product" button
3. Fill in the product details:
   - Product Name
   - SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)
   - Selling Price
   - Critical Stock Level
4. Click "Save" to add the product

#### Viewing Products

1. Go to the "Products" section
2. Browse through the list of products
3. Use the search functionality to find specific products

#### Editing Products

1. Navigate to the "Products" section
2. Find the product you want to edit
3. Click the "Edit" button
4. Make the necessary changes
5. Click "Save" to update the product

#### Deleting Products

1. Navigate to the "Products" section
2. Find the product you want to delete
3. Click the "Delete" button
4. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog

### Supplier Management

#### Adding New Suppliers

1. Navigate to the "Suppliers" section
2. Click the "Add Supplier" button
3. Fill in the supplier details:
   - Company Name
   - Contact Person
   - Phone Number
   - Email Address
4. Click "Save" to add the supplier

#### Viewing Suppliers

1. Go to the "Suppliers" section
2. Browse through the list of suppliers

#### Editing Suppliers

1. Navigate to the "Suppliers" section
2. Find the supplier you want to edit
3. Click the "Edit" button
4. Make the necessary changes
5. Click "Save" to update the supplier

#### Deleting Suppliers

1. Navigate to the "Suppliers" section
2. Find the supplier you want to delete
3. Click the "Delete" button
4. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog

### Purchase Order Management

#### Creating Purchase Orders

1. Navigate to the "Purchase Orders" section
2. Click the "Create Purchase Order" button
3. Select a supplier from the dropdown
4. Add items to the purchase order:
   - Select a product
   - Enter quantity
   - Enter unit price
5. Review the total amount
6. Click "Save" to create the purchase order

#### Confirming Purchase Orders

1. Navigate to the "Purchase Orders" section
2. Find the purchase order you want to confirm
3. Click the "Confirm" button
4. The order status will change to "Confirmed"

#### Cancelling Purchase Orders

1. Navigate to the "Purchase Orders" section
2. Find the purchase order you want to cancel
3. Click the "Cancel" button
4. Confirm the cancellation in the dialog box
5. The order status will change to "Cancelled"

## User Roles and Permissions

The system supports two user roles with different permissions:

### Administrator

- Full access to all modules
- Can manage users and their permissions
- Can perform all actions on products, suppliers, and purchase orders

### Operational User

- Limited access to core modules
- Can view and manage products, suppliers, and purchase orders
- Cannot manage user accounts or system settings

## Reporting

The system provides basic reporting capabilities:

### Inventory Reports

- View current stock levels
- Generate reports on low stock items
- Export inventory data to CSV format

### Purchase Order Reports

- View purchase order history
- Filter by supplier or date range
- Export purchase order data to CSV format

## Settings

Administrators can configure system-wide preferences such as:
- Default currency

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Unable to Login

- Ensure your username and password are correct
- Check that your account is active
- Contact your system administrator if you continue to experience issues

#### Slow Performance

- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Clear your browser cache
- Try accessing the system during off-peak hours

#### Data Not Saving

- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page and try again
- Contact technical support if the problem persists
